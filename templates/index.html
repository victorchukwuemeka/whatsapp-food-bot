How it works
Four steps. Zero apps.
Everything happens inside WhatsApp — the app you already have.
1

Send a message

Open WhatsApp, message Brida, and tell us what you want to eat.

2

Confirm your order

Our restaurant agent shows your items and a fixed food total.

3

Negotiate delivery

A rider agent proposes a fee. You counter, they respond — until you both agree.

4

Order dispatched

Confirm the final summary and your food is on its way.

Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2025

Brida ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our WhatsApp-based food ordering service.

Information we collect

When you interact with Brida via WhatsApp, we may collect your WhatsApp phone number, order history and preferences, delivery address if provided, and chat history within the service session.

How we use your information

We use your data solely to process and fulfill your food orders, improve our AI agents and service quality, and communicate order updates with you via WhatsApp.

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Order details may be shared with our restaurant and delivery partners solely to fulfill your order.

Data retention

Your session and order data is retained for up to 90 days for service improvement purposes, after which it is anonymized or deleted.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at the details below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Brida after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
